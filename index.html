About Event

On 08.10.1871 the "Continental-Caoutchouc- und Gutta-Percha Compagnie" was founded in Hanover, Germany. From 250 employees in 1879 it grew thanks to internationalization and technological leadership via 13.000 employees in 1914 to nowadays 236.000 employees in 561 locations in 58 countries worldwide. Continental is the 2nd biggest automotive supplier in the world.

We would like to thank our best customers for the successful cooperation in the last year and together with Kislev Transportation LTD we invite you to celebrate Continental's 150th birthday together.

We will launch the Continental SportContact'" 7 which represents the latest technological leap.

The Continental Sport Contact" 7 is designed to be an ultra high performance on road tyre, which can also be used at race tracks. You will have the chance to experience the outstanding German Engineering in MotorCity on selected AUDI vehicles.

Continental will present to you the wide portfolio of tyres and explain the latest technology in truck, motorcycle and bicycle tyres.

The future starts now. Continental is digitalizing the tyre management to continuously improve the performance of our tyres and provide the lowest overall driving costs to you. Together with our Israeli partner Traffilog we will present you the latest innovation of a digitalized fleet management.

We are looking forward to spend with you the day with Continental.
